My name is Corey Wood.

I also go by corky or corkybeta.

I would describe myself first as a Christian, then a GNU/Linux user, then a drummer and music obsessive.

I must admit, I can't maintain a blog. My self motivation is minimal and I can barely keep a private, daily diary going but I would like my domain to display a single static page about me. I'm not a javascript fan but don't even have the desire to build something beautiful in Pico CMS about me. I love good typography and I think this will serve just as well. This site (forked and modified) is:

  • Lightweight and loads fast;
  • Fits on all of your screens;
  • Looks the same in all of your awful browsers;
  • Accessible to everyone that visits my site;
  • Legible with nice line spacing and soft contrast;
  • Designed to put a focus on my words with beautiful typography.
